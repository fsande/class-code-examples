Attendance at practical sessions

The exercises proposed as practices are evaluated on Tuesdays and Wednesdays from 12:00-14:00h. In these sessions, students are proposed programming exercises and the exercise proposed the previous week is reviewed. If on any occasion it happens that they cannot attend the practice evaluation session, the following protocol will be followed:

Show details
  • Inform the teacher of the incident in question as soon as possible.
  • Be aware that missing a practice does not have to be a problem, since that grade can be recovered with other grades in the remaining practices. What would be a problem is repeated non-attendance.
  • If it is a formally justified reason, you can change your correction session to the other weekly session: if it is a Wednesday group, you can change to Tuesday and vice versa.
  • No changes will be made to correct practices outside the week for which they have been planned: each week's practices are evaluated exclusively in the corresponding sessions listed in the course timetable.
